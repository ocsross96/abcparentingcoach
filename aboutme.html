Meet Amalia

Amalia is a highly dedicated and supportive Health Professional who is passionate about assisting parents with the sometimes challenging task of parenting.

Her many years experience within the Child Health setting of various countries and cultures, equip her with strong insight into the complexities of raising happy and healthy children.

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Master of Science in Health Promotion King’s College London, London, United Kingdom
  • Bachelor of Science in Nursing McMaster University, Hamilton, ON.,Canada.
  • Current certificate of competence as registered nurse from the NMC (Nursing and Midwifery Council)
  • UNICEF BFI (The Baby Friendly Initiative) Training: a) Train the Trainer, and b) Breastfeeding and Relationship Building (Health Visiting)
  • Certified Infant Massage Instructor (CIMI) with the International Association of Infant Massage (UK Chapter)
  • BSc Hons (Top-up) Specialist Community Public Health (Health Visiting)
