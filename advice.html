Advice

On this page you will find specific information about the service that Amalia can provide

Overview

The role of the Parenting Coach is about the promotion of health. The service is offered to antenatal mothers, mothers of young babies, and parents with children of different age groups.

The service can be utilized by parents to provide training for their child minders and nannies, as well as by parents wishing to access information about Teen Health and issues related to this age group.

How does the service work?

Once your email is received, expect a call back within 24 hours to further discuss matters and arrange for a home visit. Please note, the 1st visit will be an initial consultation to establish specific needs and goals of the parents.

Home visits will include assessment, goal setting, goal implementation, and provision of up to date information pamphlets and reading materials.

Home visits can be arranged for evenings (after 6:30 PM) and on the weekends.

Home visits will only operate out of the following boroughs within London: Kensington/Chelsea, Hammersmith/Fulham, City, Richmond Upon Thames, Wandsworth, and Westminster (with the exception of the Bayswater area)
