Advice

On this page you will find specific information about the service that Amalia can provide

Overview

The role of the Parenting Coach is about the promotion of child development and long term health, as well as that of positive parenting.The service is offered to groups of: parents of young babies, parents with children of different age groups including Teens, and members of staff dealing with children.

The service can also be utilized by: parents wishing to provide training for their own personal child minders and nannies; and by companies dealing with child care/development to increase the knowledge of their staff.

How does the service work?

Once your email is received, expect a reply within 48 hours to further discuss specific needs and goals and make necessary arrangements.

Presentation/Course can be arranged for the evening (after 5:30 PM), and for the weekend.

Presentation/Course will only operate within London, U.K.
